About Us

Bookland — A Home for Readers

Nestled in the heart of Jhamsikhel, Lalitpur — just a short walk from Chicken Station — Bookland is more than a bookstore. It is a sanctuary for curious minds, a gathering place for students, writers, and lifelong learners alike. We stock titles from over 80 international and national publishers, curated across eight unique reading chambers.

Whether you seek the latest international bestseller, a rare Nepali literary gem, or an academic textbook, Bookland has it waiting for you on our shelves.
